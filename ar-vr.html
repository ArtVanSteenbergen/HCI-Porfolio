AR & VR

Assignments

VR Car concept

Imagine sitting in a self driving car with a headset on and controlling a virtual car. While the car itself is driving along the highway obeying the law. You can use the driver inputs to have fun racing in a virtual world. In car entertainment is going to be very important in the future. Instead of just getting bored behind the wheel or even start driving the car yourself again. You can have heaps of fun while driving/getting driven long distances. This would only be possible with level 5 autonomy of course.

VR Therapy concept

People with Acrophobia (The Fear of Heights) could benefit from training with walking on heights in VR. That way they know that the can't fall or jump in the deep. To help them cross the narrow bridge we insentivece them with achievements and some kind of gamification element. This way the have a fun way to reduce stress at heights. After doing the experiment in VR they may be less affead in real life. Or you could guid them in a dark room where they can't see that they are acually walking at height. However, I think that would result in some insurence clames.

AR Public transport concept

Wouldn't it be nice if you could see where the doors of the train would stop when a train arrives. Trains allready have pre defined spots where they come to a stop. So, it wouldn't be that hard to make a virtual train in AR on your phone in the NS Reisplanner app. When you're waiting on the platform and you hold your phone up, it would show you where the train stops with a virtual representation. It could also show how crouded the train cars are before it arrives.
