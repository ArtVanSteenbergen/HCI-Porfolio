Artificial Creatures

Some algorityms create such creature like movements and behavour that we think that they are really alive. It doesn't even matter if it's on a screen or if it's a complete animatronic machine. People will relate to them. In the lecture about Artificical Creatures I got really exited about making my own with my arduino. That's why I chose it as my favourite topic for the lab weeks. It's like creating a living thing without the hassle of cleaning up dirty diapers.
